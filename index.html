Marcela Alencar

Hi, I'm Marcela Alencar! I am a Brazilian artist who is passionate about music, books and art. Drawing has been present in my life since I was a child and I remember spending hours just practicing different drawing techniques, all of this has always been fascinating to me.

My art is adorable, captivating and I always like to add a touch of fantasy so people have a fantastic experience when they look at my work. As my illustrations are primarily created digitally, they can be applied to a wide variety of media and used across platforms including animation.
